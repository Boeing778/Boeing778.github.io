SDT Excercise - version 3

Instructions

This exercise is designed to allow you to collect data under a specific signal-to-noise ratio.

  • When you click on the 'Start' button below, the screen will change and you will see a 'Response' button in the center at the bottom of the screen.
  • A timer will be displayed next to it, showing you the time in seconds out of the total. This exercise will last for 1800 seconds (i.e., 30 minutes).
  • Before beginning, make sure you have enough time to finish. You can Pause the program, and hit play once you are ready to resume.
  • The screen will display 3 elements at a time for approximately 1 second and then they will disappear and the next set of 3 will appear.
  • Any time during the 1800 seconds that you see the letter Y on the screen press the response button.
  • When the counter gets to 1800, the program will stop. Click on the Analyze button.
